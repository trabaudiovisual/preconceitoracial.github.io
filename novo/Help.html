LAW No. 7.716 / 89

Defines crimes resulting from race and color bias.

Art. 1 The crimes resulting from discrimination or prejudice of race, color, ethnicity, religion or national origin shall be punished under this Law.

Art. 3. To prevent or impede the access of someone, duly qualified, to any position of the direct or indirect administration, as well as of the concessionaires of public services.

Penalty: imprisonment from two (2) to five (5) years.

Art. 20. Practice, induce or incite discrimination or prejudice of race, color, ethnicity, religion or national origin.

Penalty: imprisonment of 1 (one) to 3 (three) years and fine.
